How to Play

Welcome to Advanced Tic Tac Toe! The game is simple, but requires strategy. Here’s how to play:

Players take turns placing X or O on the board. The goal is to line up three marks of same size in a row, column, or diagonal.

Game Rules:

  • Objective: Align three symbols (X or O) of same size in a row, column, or diagonal to win.
  • Players: Player 1 uses "X", and Player 2 uses "O".
  • Select Symbol Size: Choose the size of the symbol you want to place (Small, Medium, or Large).
  • Making a Move: Click on an empty cell to place your symbol. You cannot place a smaller symbol on a larger one.
  • Switch Turns: The game automatically switches turns after each move.
  • Winning: A player wins by getting three of their symbols of same size in a row, column, or diagonal.
  • Draw Condition: If all cells are filled without a winner, it’s a draw.
