How to Play

Welcome to Advanced Tic Tac Toe!

  • Objective: Align three symbols of different sizes (1 small, 1 medium, 1 large) in a row, column, or diagonal to win.
  • Players: Player 1 uses "X" and Player 2 uses "O".
  • Select Symbol Size: Choose the size (Small, Medium, Large) to place.
  • Move Rules: You can place a larger symbol over a smaller one but cannot do the reverse.
  • Winning: Complete a row, column, or diagonal with different sizes to win.
