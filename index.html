How to Play

Welcome to Tic Tac Toe!

Players take turns placing X or O on the board. The goal is to line up three marks in a row, column, or diagonal.

Game Rules:

  • You can place a Small, Medium, or Large X/O on the board.
  • Player 1 uses "X" (green), and Player 2 uses "O" (blue).
  • You cannot overwrite existing marks unless it's a larger size.
  • The game ends when one player gets 3 in a row or both players run out of moves.
